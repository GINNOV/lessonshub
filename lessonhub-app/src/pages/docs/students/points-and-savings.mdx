# Points & Savings System

LessonHub uses a dual-currency system to track your progress: **Points** for the leaderboard and **Savings (Euros)** for your personal "bank."

## How Points Work

Points determine your rank on the leaderboard. They reward **quality** and **speed**.

### Earning Points
- **Graded Assignments:** You earn points for every assignment your teacher grades. Higher scores mean more points.
- **Extra Points:** Teachers can award bonus points for going above and beyond.
- **Gold Stars:** A special award that gives a massive point boost (calculated based on the star's value).

### Losing Points (Penalties)
- **Extensions:** Asking for a deadline extension costs **200 points**.
- **Composer Retries:** In Composer lessons, using extra tries to solve a puzzle costs **50 points per try**.
- **News Article low taps:** Submitting with **fewer than 6 taps** deducts points equal to **3Ã— the lesson price**.
