# Hub Guides

Hub Guides deliver on-demand practice between assignments. Think of them as LessonHub’s curated library of extra learning material.

## Access Levels

- **Paying Students:** You get access to the full catalog. Look for the "Hub Guides" tab on your dashboard.
- **Free Students:** You can access specific “Free access” guides. To unlock the full library, visit **Profile → Billing** to upgrade.
- **Teachers:** You can build and manage guides using the Guide Creator.

## Guide Cards

When you browse guides, here is what you see on each card:

- **Preview Image:** Custom artwork helps you identify the topic.
- **Badge:** A specific “Hub Guide” icon.
- **Description:** A brief summary of what you will learn.
- **Difficulty:** Filled bars indicate how advanced the guide is.
- **Updated Date:** Shows when the content was last refreshed.
- **Free Access Pill:** Labeled clearly if the guide is available to everyone.

## The Player Experience

Clicking a guide opens the **Guide Player**:
- **Clean Layout:** Distraction-free reading with a hero image.
- **Instructions:** Step-by-step details on how to use the guide.
- **Media:** Audio tracks, attachments, and lyrics (for songs) are built right in.

## For Teachers

- **Creation:** Use the Guide Creator to build multi-step learning cards.
- **Bulk Import:** You can import sessions from CSV files for faster setup.
- **Visibility:** Toggle `guideIsFreeForAll` to `true` to make a guide available to all students (e.g., for seasonal promotions).
