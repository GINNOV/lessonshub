# Getting Started

LessonHub is a calm workspace for students, teachers, and admins. Set up once, then stay focused on the work that matters.

## Access Requirements

- **Browser:** Latest Safari, Chrome, or Edge. Mobile Safari is fully supported.
- **Account:** New students are invited by their teacher. Teachers and admins receive credentials from LessonHub staff.
- **Network:** The app is optimized for low-latency connections. A stable broadband or LTE link is enough.

## Sign In

1. Visit `https://lessonhub.app`.
2. Select **Sign in**.
3. Enter the email and password from your welcome email.
4. If prompted, confirm via email verification.

## Roles at a Glance

| Role | Landing View | Key Abilities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Student** | `/my-lessons` | Complete assignments, explore Hub Guides, track progress. |
| **Teacher** | `/dashboard` | Assign lessons, grade work, manage students. |
| **Admin** | `/dashboard` | Manage users, system settings, emails, and cron jobs. |

## Navigation Basics

- **Top Navigation:** Click your avatar to access your profile, sign out, or view account status.
- **Tabs:** Use the pill-style tabs (e.g., “Lessons / Hub Guides”) to switch views. The active tab is highlighted.
- **Cards:** Content is organized into cards. Hover (desktop) or tap (mobile) to interact.

## Essential First Step

After your first sign-in, go to **Profile → Update Profile**.
*   Set your **Timezone** (crucial for accurate deadlines).
*   Upload a **Photo**.
*   Confirm your **Preferred Name**.
